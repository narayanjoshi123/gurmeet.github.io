DAV UNIVERSITY JALANDHAR

*Pioneering history *Glorious present *Promising future

DAV university is a private universiy in jalandhar,Punjab,Founded in 2013 under aegis of DAV trust. DAV university traces it's roots to the legacy of Dayanand Anglo-Vedic Schools Systems that has been reforming and redefining India's educational scenerio for 128 years.The univsersity is the culmination of the movement that started with the foundation of the first DAV institute to propagate the ideals of the religious and social reformer Swami Dayanand Saraswati in Lahore om 1 June 1886.The university has adopted 5 surrounding villages and doing public welfare there.
